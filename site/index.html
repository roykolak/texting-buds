Texting Buddies

Find a random texting buddy

Good news, life isn't that important. Text w/ a random buddy. Standard texting rates apply, but you already knew that.

Send a text message to this number

630-599-8662

And you'll be connected to someone who is looking for a random texting buddy just like you.

Send these commands by SMS

#next
Finds you a text buddy. If there are no buddies available at the moment, you'll be added to the queue.
#stop
Instantly stop all buddy chatting and end your interactions with TextingBuds.
#block
Use this to block your buddy if they are spamming you or being a jerk.
#help
Get all the commands available with their descriptions.

Keep three things in mind

  1. 1

    3 blocks and you're banned

    If you are blocked 3 times, you will be banned from Texting Buddies. This is to make sure the buddies to text with are nice people.

  2. 2

    Stop at any time

    Just send #stop and you will stop receiving all text messages from buddies. It's easy to start and even easier to quit.

  3. 3

    Completely anonymous

    Your phone number is never exposed. All texting happens through the Texting Buddies number, 630-599-8662. Be nice and random.

What exactly is stored here?

Your phone number is the only personal information that is stored. Your phone number is the bare minimium that needs to be stored in order for this project to work.

Your phone number will never, ever be shared.

The conversations between buddies are not stored. Those conversations are personal to you and buddies.
